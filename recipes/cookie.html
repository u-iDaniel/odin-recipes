Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Yummy. Chewy. Gooey. What's not to love? Also goes well with milk!

A light snack for any time of the day. Yields 48 cookies. Done in 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Make sure butter is softened and eggs are at room temperature. Preheat oven to 350 F (175 ⁰C)
  2. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth. Should take around 4 minutes
  3. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes
  8. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy!
